iOS Installation (Safari):

  1. Open this website in Safari browser
  2. Tap the Share button (□↗) at the bottom
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" to confirm

The app will appear on your home screen with the conference logo!

Android Installation (Chrome/Edge):

  1. Open this website in Chrome or Edge browser
  2. Look for the "Add to Home Screen" prompt, or
  3. Tap the browser menu (⋮) → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
  4. Tap "Add" or "Install" to confirm

The app will work offline and launch like a native app!
